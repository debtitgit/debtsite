Services


FOR SECTOR ECONOMY

We troubleshoot a wide variety of pain points for the sector/economy whole some of which are, The funding gap in the economy as a whole and financial inclusion setback. We minimize the information gap and reduce the mismatch among borrowers and lenders which makes non existence of a comprehensive market place for loans. The traditional appraisal system which is bungling in identifying potential stressed assets.

FOR BORROWERS

We ensure that with the one time documentation we guarantees that borrower can approach to multiple lenders. We provide interest rate negotiation through bidding among banks/lenders that ensures that the borrower gets loan at the least interest rate. We ensure that the borrower is not bothered by lenders through calls or direct approach by ensuring that all approaches are through the portal alone.

FOR LENDERS

We provide the right tools for a 360° appraisal of borrowers, take care of documentation from KYC to CIBIL and valuation to title search and provide a credit appraisal report to banks based on their credit appraisal criteria. We provide not just lead for loans but provide all the information that the lender would need to decide on lending to the borrower. We save the duplication of cost by banks.

We are not a web aggregator. We are an intermediary in its truest sense.

We do not sell data. We render a service. An expert one at that.

Our Solution

About Us

DebtIt is a loan market place which brings all different types of lenders and borrowers under one roof/platform. DebtIt Objective is convert the loan market from a seller's market to a buyer's market. Instead of you approaching banks, interested lenders (banks/NBFC) will browse and approach you through the portal and compete (bid) among themselves to lend to you. Here you just buy loans.

DebtIt shall be the virtual marketplace for credit which will bring together all the market forces on one platform giving customer the choice for credit and Financial institutions the means to solicit business under bear and bull model, in a cost effective and efficient manner. It will provide the cheapest loans to customers, increase the business of the banks and create wealth thereby for all stakeholders.

Establish a bidding platform for mobility of funds where interest rate is determined on real time basis based on demand, supply, tenure, risk, mortgage and collateral offered.

With 170 banks (Public, Private,& foreign) 3000 cooperative banks, 12000 NBFC and innumerable number of unorganised sector lenders, (whom we plan to bring together on the platform) competition will be at its best.
